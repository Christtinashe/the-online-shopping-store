Tomas Orlej

Slovakia

View Profile

I have interested in drawing since childhood. When I was 5, i painted walls in our house by pencil. I drawed my whole name backward. My parents were angry, but surprised, that I know write my name. Later my parents and my older brothers decided, that I must go to the building construction high schol and university. I always want to be an artist. When I was 16, I started with graffiti and streetart, but in 20 I was busted by a police for a illegal painting in the street. Since this bad experience i decided for a legaly way. I changed my pseudo-name and I focused on streetart (stickers, posters, canvases, stencils and roller-painting). Im making colorful adhesive foils stickers since 2002. I started painting canvases mostly based on the tangible things. Usually they were favourite things or things starting with the same letter. Words were in Slovak language. Now I am working in building construction company in Wien as a building planner. But my heart beats for an art.
